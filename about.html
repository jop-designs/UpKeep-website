About UpKeep

A cultural shift in how we value bikes

UpKeep bicycles is an open source community for people who want longer bike life. We focus on parts that are often ignored until the whole bike feels degraded. Instead of waiting for breakdown, we fix value early.

What UpKeep is

A shared library of practical bicycle part designs and workshop methods. Not step-by-step repair manuals, but adaptable files and local support to help people make meaningful upgrades.

What UpKeep is not

We are not trying to replace bike mechanics or overcomplicate maintenance. We focus on approachable parts that influence comfort, aesthetics, and emotional connection to a bike.
