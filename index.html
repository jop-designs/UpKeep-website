The Problem

Bikes Are Scrapped Long Before They Truly Fail

Small signs of neglect build up until a perfectly repairable bike starts to feel worthless. UpKeep focuses on the parts that shape everyday dignity, safety, and desire to keep riding.

160,000

Bikes turned into scrap each year that often need only manageable part repairs.

150,000

Additional bikes sit neglected because comfort, looks, and utility parts are worn out.

1 shift

From replacing whole bikes to upgrading key parts while the bike still works.
