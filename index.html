The Problem

How UpKeep Works

A new way to take care of your bike

  1. 1. Find a workshop You find a local workshop that is part of the UpKeep Community
  2. 2. Make parts & repairs Here you can make your own bicycle parts and get help with repairs if you need it.
  3. 3. Customize your bike You can make your bike nicer to look at, more functional and more unique
  4. 4. Enjoy the ride Appreciate your bike and use it for longer
