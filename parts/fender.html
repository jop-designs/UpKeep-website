Under construction

This part is still under development by the community. We are trying to find a way to make fabricating a fender fun and pleasurable. If you would like to participate and join our open-source community, please send an email.

Development Updates

Latest: Rolling the Fender (14-02-2026)

Initial prototypes explored hand-cranked DIY roller machines to form metal fenders. After facing challenges with edge buckling, development has shifted towards exploring thermoformed PETG strips and "airplane wing" structural frames.
