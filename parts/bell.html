Development Updates

Latest: The challenges of DIY metal forming (10-02-2026)

We started by trying to press sheet metal into a traditional bell shape using 3D printed moulds. However, the edges wrinkled, and expert advice confirmed that DIY crash-forming is very difficult for this shape. To keep the project accessible, we are now exploring alternative geometries like copper pipe bells!
