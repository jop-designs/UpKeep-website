Host a Workshop

Bring UpKeep to your city

UpKeep workshops help people build useful bike parts before bikes reach a breaking point. You provide a space and local energy, we provide an open framework that is easy to adopt and improve.

Why host UpKeep

Most riders can handle practical upgrades if the environment feels welcoming. Hosting a workshop grows local repair confidence, keeps bicycles in use longer, and builds neighborhood skill-sharing.

How workshops work

Sessions are structured around one part focus at a time. Participants arrive with a bike, select an open file, and leave with a usable upgrade plus the know-how to maintain it.
