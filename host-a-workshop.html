Bring UpKeep to your city

UpKeep workshops help people build useful parts for their bikes so we can keep using them for longer. You provide the space and the tools and we provide the resources and a community that cares about bicycles.

Why host UpKeep

As a UpKeep host you're helping to create more sustainable relationship with bicycles in your city. By hosting part making workshop you will help to grow repair confidence and extend the life of city bikes in your area.

How do workshops work

Sessions are structured around one part at a time. Participants arrive with a bike, they make their desired part, and leave with a bike that is uniquely their own.
